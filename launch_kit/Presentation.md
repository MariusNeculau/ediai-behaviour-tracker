# EDI AI Behaviour Tracker
## Pitch Presentation — Irish Special Education Schools

---

## The Problem

**SEN schools spend hours every week on paperwork that should take minutes.**

- Behaviour incidents logged on paper forms or fragmented spreadsheets
- No single view of a child's patterns across triggers, times, and interventions
- Epilepsy and seizure records kept separately from behaviour logs — clinical risk
- Generating a report for NCSE or HSE reviews takes hours of manual compilation
- New staff have no quick access to a child's protocol or seizure history

> *"Staff are filing incident reports at the end of the day from memory — data quality suffers, patterns go unnoticed."*

---

## The Solution: EDI AI Behaviour Tracker

**A single, secure, offline system that replaces paper logs, spreadsheets, and disconnected files.**

### Core Capabilities

- **Incident Logging** — log any behaviour incident in under 60 seconds: type, severity, trigger, interventions, outcome, staff member
- **Seizure Module** — dedicated epilepsy log with seizure type, duration, position, protocol compliance, medication, and post-ictal notes
- **Therapy Sessions** — supervisors set clinical goals; therapists record trial results; accuracy tracked automatically
- **Instant Reports** — Individual Child, Class Summary, and Whole School PDF reports generated on demand
- **Emergency Protocol Card** — one-click printable card per child with seizure history and first-aid steps
- **Data Exports** — incident and seizure data exported to CSV for external analysis or archiving

### How It Works

1. Staff logs an incident or seizure from any browser on the school network
2. Data is saved instantly to a secure local database — **nothing leaves the school premises**
3. Supervisors view dashboards, spot patterns, and generate reports in seconds
4. NCSE review coming up? Print the Individual Child PDF with one click

## Key Benefits

### For Class Teachers & SNAs
- Log incidents in under 60 seconds — no end-of-day paper pile
- Seizure form auto-appears when needed — no missing clinical fields

### For Principals & SENCOs
- School-wide dashboard: incidents today, active crises, staff on duty
- Behaviour patterns across time of day and day of week — built-in
- NCSE-ready PDF reports covering incidents, therapy progress, and seizure history

### For Clinical Safety
- Seizure Emergency Protocol Card — printable, always up to date, based on live data
- Protocol compliance tracking — know at a glance which incidents followed the seizure protocol
- Correlation alerts: therapy sessions flag if a child had incidents the same day

### For GDPR Compliance
- **All data stored locally** — on your school's own machine, never in the cloud
- No accounts, no subscriptions, no data sharing with third parties
- Fully compliant with GDPR and the Irish Data Protection Act 2018
